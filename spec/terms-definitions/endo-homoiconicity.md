[[def: endo-homoiconicity, endo-homoiconicity, Endo-homoiconicity, endo-homoiconic]]

~ A deepening of classical homoiconicity that establishes a strict structural isomorphism across the full representational substrate — unifying types, invariants, and execution paths into a canonical form through which data, configurations, function calls, errors, and behavioural contracts are natively composed, evaluated, and resolved.
